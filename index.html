About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Bahria University, Islamabad, Pakistan. I hold a Ph.D. in Computer Science & Technology from Nanjing University of Science & Technology, China. My research focuses on advanced machine learning and deep learning models for bioinformatics, computational biology, and disease diagnosis.
